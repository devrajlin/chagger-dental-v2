Invisalign®

All You Need To Know About Aligner Therapy

Our dental professionals take pride in building beautiful, confident smiles. Our team of orthodontic specialists can work with you to determine a treatment plan that best suits your needs.

Whether you have gapped teeth, crowded teeth, or want to transform your crooked teeth into straight teeth, our dentists are highly specialized in Cosmetic Dentistry. We are here to help complete a smile assessment, discuss the types of braces available, and help you determine the best treatment options to create your perfect smile.

Chagger Dental also offers aligner therapy, also known as invisible braces! We offer AURRA, Invisalign, SureSmile, and ClearCorrect. Let us help you decide which option is best for you.

Advantages of Aligner Therapy

1

Freedom From Metal Orthodontic Braces

One of the greatest advantages to using aligner therapy vs standard metal braces involves the aspect of visibility. Wearing braces that are transparent, removable and virtually invisible to see is a positive alternative for teens and adults wanting discretion when undergoing orthodontic treatment.

2

Being Able To Eat What You Want

A problem associated with traditional braces is the limitations on what you can and cannot eat. Many foods we enjoyed and took for granted before having braces are not able to be eaten, as many hard foods can cause breaking and damage to the braces. If you opt for aligner therapy, you can eat what you want and still get straight teeth. You no longer need to be concerned about food breaking brackets. Invisalign, SureSmile and ClearCorrect aligners are removable! As a result, to continue enjoying meal times during your aligner therapy you simply remove your aligner trays prior to indulging.

3

Spending More Time Outside Of The Dentist's Office

As much as we enjoy seeing our patients, we value your time. Aligner therapy allows for longer time between follow-up appointments, every four to six weeks compared to every two weeks with wire braces.

4

Costs of Invisalign Ontario, SureSmile, and ClearCorrect

Another benefit to using Invisalign braces is their cost-effective element. While the cost of aligner therapy does vary, they can result in significant cost savings when compared to their traditional counterparts. As aligner therapy offers a removable option, breakage, repair and adjustments are minimal, resulting in less appointments. In fact, upon beginning your Invisalign treatment you will be fitted with a series of advanced clear aligners designed to slowly shift your teeth throughout the course of your treatment until they’ve reached the optimal position. Upon your check-ups, every four to six weeks, the Chagger Dental team will assess your teeth’s progress and determine when you are ready for your aligner changes.

5

Easy Maintenance

Wire braces require a high maintenance oral care routine of specialized brushing and flossing to keep them clean. Invisalign, SureSmile and ClearCorrect are not adhered directly to your teeth. They are simply a series of clear trays that are designed specifically for your mouth using align technology. These advanced clear aligners can be removed for easy cleaning. You simply wear your aligners throughout the day and remove them for eating, sleeping and your at home oral hygiene routine. The option to remove them for brushing and flossing makes maintenance easy. No longer are you worried about navigating the wires of traditional braces, you can continue to brush with simplicity.

Call your local Chagger Dental office today to see if Invisalign, SureSmile or ClearCorrect is right for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many hours a day should I wear my aligners? expand_more

For optimal results, aligners should be worn 20 to 22 hours per day, removing them only for eating, drinking, brushing, and flossing.

Will the treatment be painful? expand_more

Most patients experience a sense of pressure rather than pain. This is a sign the aligners are working to move your teeth into their desired positions.

Is Invisalign suitable for all ages? expand_more

Yes, from Invisalign First for children to treatments for adults of all ages, the technology is versatile enough to handle almost any orthodontic journey.
